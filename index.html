About me

I am passionate about using data to understand complex global issues and to help decision-makers make better decisions.

Currently, I work as a senior data analyst at the ONE Campaign, a global advocacy organization. I build data-driven content and tools to raise awareness about global issues and support our advocacy efforts. Check out some of our work around these issues.

I am originally from Sardinia, but I have lived in many places around the world. I am currently based in Paris, France.
