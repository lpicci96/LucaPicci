About me

I am passionate about using data and data visualisation understand complex global issues and to make decisions for a better future.

I work on issues ranging from global health to gender equality and climate change. I build modern data tools and Python packages to make global development data more accessible. I make data visualisations to clearly explain complex, multifaceted, and often interelated issues in global development.

I am from Sardinia, and currently live in Paris. In my free time I am a fiercly competitive tennis player or out enjoying fresh air on my bike.
